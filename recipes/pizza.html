Pizza

This is how to make a pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Basil Garlic

Steps

  1. Knead the dough
  2. Spread the sauce
  3. Add the cheese and pepperoni
  4. For extra tastiness, add basil garlic (Thank for CWISSY for this amazing tip)
  5. Bake at 450 degrees for 13 minutes
