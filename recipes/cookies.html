Cookies

This is how to make chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • Cookie Dough
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Add chocolate chips to the cookie dough
  2. Butter the pan
  3. Cut out circular shapes from the cookie dough
  4. Alternatively, you can roll the cookie dough into balls
  5. Place the cookie dough balls on the baking pan
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 min
